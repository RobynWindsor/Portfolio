About Me

My journey into the colorful world of web development started with a fascination for how a bunch of lines of code could turn into beautiful, interactive playgrounds. I realized that with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript as my trusty companions, I could craft digital experiences that make people go Wow! Design isn't just what it looks like; it's how it works. I'm a firm believer in this principle. My approach to front-end development is design-driven, with a focus on creating intuitive, user-centered interfaces that seamlessly merge form and function. Hello, and welcome to my profile. I am a London based Front-End Developer with a strong background as a Studio Manager for a high-end picture framing company. My journey into the colourful world of web development is infused with lessons from my background in picture framing. Let me tell you about my journey. Studio Manager I am a dedicated and accomplished Studio Manager with 10 years’ experience in the high- end picture framing industry. Working with art fairs, galleries, and renowned artists in London and internationally. Overseeing the entire process while ensuring that each piece of art is treated with utmost care and precision. This unique experience has provided me with invaluable lessons in project management, client communication, a keen eye for aesthetics and attention to detail. Values that I carry into my coding. Front-End Developer My journey as a Front-End Developer started out of a passion to bridge the gap between design and technology. I am passionate about crafting digital experiences that are not only visually stunning but also seamlessly functional. With a keen eye for aesthetics and an in-depth understanding of user experiences my goal is to create applications that not only look amazing but also provide an intuitive and enjoyable user experience.
