Before Front-End

For over a decade, I immersed myself in the picture framing industry, evolving from a junior consultant to a studio manager, lead consultant, and project manager. My journey has taken me through various roles in workshops, honing the crafts and skills needed for high-end bespoke picture framing.

I've collaborated with prestigious galleries, artists, art fairs, collectors, and interior designers. My role demanded a strong grasp of design, aesthetics, functionality, budgeting, market trends, and above all, problem-solving. Ensuring every frame's perfection has been my mission, requiring impeccable collaboration and communication skills to unite cross-functional teams on diverse projects.

The collaboration and communication skills I have developed has proven to be the key to my success when bringing cross-functional teams and individuals together on small and large projects.

2023 Summer Romance

Fast forward to 2023 where my love for front-end web development

My desire to continuously learn, create and problem solve was instantly ignited with my first few lines of code, as I built my first basic landing page in HTML and CSS.It felt like magic and as if I had discovered a whole new world full of endless possibilities. Which is exactly what it was!

Front-end development allows me to blend creativity with technology. I get to transform static design concepts into interactive and visually appealing websites and applications. The ability to create beautiful and user-friendly interfaces is incredibly rewarding.

My journey into front-end development coincided with a deepening interest in climate tech and reducing carbon emissions. I believe technology can be a powerful force for positive change in addressing climate change. Integrating sustainable practices into web development, such as optimizing performance to reduce energy consumption, aligns with my commitment to environmental responsibility.

I'm eager to leverage my skills in front-end development to contribute to projects that promote sustainability and combat climate change. By harnessing technology for good, we can create a greener, more sustainable future for generations to come.
