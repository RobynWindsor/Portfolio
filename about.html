About Me

Front-end development allows me to combine creativity with technology, transforming static designs into interactive and visually engaging websites and applications. I’ve since expanded my skillset to include JavaScript and Python, adding dynamic functionality to my work. I'm proficient in frameworks and libraries like React (JSX), Bootstrap, and Tailwind CSS, which enable me to create polished, responsive designs efficiently. I also use tools like Node.js and GitHub for streamlined development workflows and solid version control, along with a foundation in API integration, SEO, responsive design, and WordPress to ensure user-friendly, high-performance websites.

Advocacy for Neurodiversity and Inclusivity

As a neurodiverse developer, I’m deeply passionate about creating technology that is inclusive and accessible for all. My own experiences shape my approach to design, and I strive to build websites and applications that embrace diverse cognitive needs and perspectives. By adhering to accessibility standards and designing with inclusivity in mind, I aim to create digital experiences that are user-friendly and welcoming for everyone.

Climate Tech and sustainability

My journey in front-end development aligns with my growing commitment to sustainability and climate tech. I’m a firm believer that technology has a pivotal role in tackling climate challenges. By integrating eco-conscious practices into web development—like performance optimization to minimize energy consumption—I aim to align my work with environmental responsibility.

I am eager to leverage my skills in front-end development for projects that prioritize sustainability, inclusivity, and advocacy. Together, we can harness technology to shape a greener, more equitable, and sustainable future.
